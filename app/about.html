Looking For Game

is the brain-child of Jason Studstill. LFG is designed to put tabletop gamers in touch with one another with the quickness. Currently games will expire 24 hours after they were created; every game you can join on this site is happening today.


Input fields

Each input is typed by you, the user. Username and password fields are case sensitive. Please be aware of this if you are on a mobile device where the OS may automatically capitalize the first letter of a sentence.
